About

Michael Walchalk is a designer who resides in the uncomfortably hot, yet wonderful city of Phoenix, Az. A graduate of Arizona State University, Michael spends his days designing for a company that specializes in hotel operating systems and his nights working on freelance projects. The services he provides include branding and identity design, logo design, illustrations, web design, posters, icons, packaging design and photo editing. He is happy to explore other avenues of design as well.

He has had the privilege of working with a wide array of great clients including ESPN, MTV and the Phoenix Suns.

Some of Michael's interests when not working include basketball, (playing, watching, cursing at the Fantasy Basketball phone app when his team isn't doing well) attempting to be funny on social media, traveling, and watching television with his cat.
